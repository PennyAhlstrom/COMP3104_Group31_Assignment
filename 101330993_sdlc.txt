DevOps in the SDLC - why it matters

DevOps enables frequent and smaller releases.

Automation reduces manual steps, speeding up deployment.

Shorter release cycles mean faster time-to-market.

Continuous monitoring and feedback reduce failures.

Automated testing ensures code quality at every stage.

Rollback strategies and CI/CD pipelines make releases more stable.
Breaks silos between developers and operations.

Improves transparency, accountability, and shared responsibility.

Encourages communication and faster problem-solving.
