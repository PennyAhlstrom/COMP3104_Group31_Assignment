George Brown College
Located in Toronto, the largest city in Canada, George Brown College offers a wide range of programs and courses. Students from around the world pursue their education here as full-time or part-time learners, with several programs available entirely online for added flexibility.

One such program is the T197 Computer Programming and Analysis program. Fully online and open to anyone living in Canada, it provides valuable educational opportunities for those in rural communities as well as for students living close to campus in downtown Toronto.
