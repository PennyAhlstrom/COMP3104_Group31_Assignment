George Brown College
Located in Toronto, the largest city in Canada, this college offers a wide range of programs and courses. Students from all over the world are getting an education here, either as full-time or part-time students. Some programs are even offered completely online for increased flexibility.

One of those programs is the T197 Computer Programming and Analysis program. Fully online and open to anyone living in Canada, it is providing education opportunities for those living far away in rural communities as well as those living right next to campus in downtown Toronto.
